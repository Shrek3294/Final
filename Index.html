Welcome to Barrio Tacos & Cantina

Barrio Tacos & Cantina is your neighborhood spot for bold, modern Mexican flavors. We specialize in street-style tacos with creative fillings, vibrant cocktails, and a lively cantina atmosphere that feels like a night out in the heart of Mexico City.

From slow-braised birria and crispy Baja fish to vegan-friendly mushroom asada, our menu is built around fresh ingredients and made-from-scratch salsas. Whether you are grabbing a quick lunch, meeting friends for margaritas, or celebrating a special occasion, we have something for everyone.

Experience the Vibe

Step inside and you will find colorful murals, warm lighting, and the sounds of sizzling tacos coming off the plancha. Our cantina offers a full bar with craft margaritas, mezcal cocktails, Mexican beers, and refreshing agua frescas.
